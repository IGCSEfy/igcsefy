Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective March 20, 2026

What IGCSEfy stores

IGCSEfy stores the settings and study activity you choose to save, including tracked subject selections, syllabus topic states, past-paper progress, and interface preferences such as theme or reduced motion.

How data is used

Saved data is used to personalise the app, restore your study state, sync progress across signed-in sessions, and support core product functions like progress export and reset.

Local and cloud storage

  • Recent searches and some interface preferences are stored in your browser using local storage.
  • If you sign in, progress data may also be synced to IGCSEfy’s Supabase backend so it can follow your account.
